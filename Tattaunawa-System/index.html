Features of the Tattaunawa System



  1. The Tattaunawa (speech recognition) system translates English voice speech to text: reduces the time spent when taking dictation.


  2. The Tattaunawa language translator allows translation of English to Nigerian native languages and vice-versa


  3. The Tattaunawa language translator helps children to easily learn the three main Nigerian native languages


  4. The Tattaunawa language translator improves and exposes a child or an adult to new vocabulary
